Inference pricing is managed by HyperClaw plans.

- Plans dashboard: [https://hyperclaw.app/dashboard/plans](https://hyperclaw.app/dashboard/plans)
- Plans API: `GET https://api.hyperclaw.app/api/plans`

## Checkout Paths

- Stripe checkout: `POST /api/stripe/{plan_id}`
- x402 checkout: `POST /api/x402/{plan_id}`

See [HyperClaw API](/hyperclaw/api) for request/response examples and behavior.
