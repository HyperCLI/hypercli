## Tiers

Get tier information:

```bash
curl https://api.hypercli.com/llm/tiers \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY"
```

### Free Tier

- Access to free models
- No credit card required
- Rate limits apply

### Rewards Tier

- Earned through platform usage
- Access to additional models
- Higher rate limits

### Paid Tier

- Full access to all models
- Highest rate limits
- Priority support

## Model Groups

Get group information:

```bash
curl https://api.hypercli.com/llm/groups \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY"
```

### free-models

Models available at no cost:

- Community models
- Open-source deployments
- Basic capabilities

### c3-models

HyperCLI's optimized model deployments:

- Optimized for performance
- Competitive pricing
- Reliable availability

### external-models

Third-party model providers:

- Premium models
- Specialized capabilities
- Pass-through pricing

## Checking Your Tier

View your current tier and access:

```bash
hyper user
hyper user -o json
```

## Rate Limits

Rate limits vary by tier:

| Tier | Requests/min | Tokens/min |
|------|--------------|------------|
| Free | Lower | Lower |
| Rewards | Medium | Medium |
| Paid | Higher | Higher |

If you hit rate limits, wait and retry or upgrade your tier.
